  ## How To Connect Bluetooth Soundbar To Roku TV


  Today in this informative article, we will guide you on how to connect Bluetooth soundbar to Roku tv. It's straightforward and easy, and you will learn in a few easy steps, so let's start it.


  **Step No: 1**


  Turn On your Roku TV and Bluetooth Soundbar.


  **Step No: 2**


  Press Home Key on your Roku TV remote.


  **Step No: 3**


  Go to the setting menu of your TCL Roku TV.


  **Step No: 4**


  Scroll down to audio and click enter.


  **Step No: 5**


  Select Audio Mode (Auto Stereo)


  After these most straightforward steps, your Bluetooth soundbar will connect to your Roku tv.


  ### How do I connect my soundbar to my TV using Bluetooth?


  Yes, you can connect your soundbar to TV using bluetooth.


  1. Turn On your TV and Soundbar.

  2. Press Home Button on your TV remote.

  3. Select Setting.

  4. Then Select Bluetooth Setting

  5. Then Select Add Device (Your TV will be in pairing mode and a list of available Bluetooth devices will appear in a few minutes.)

  6. Select the sound bar from the list and perform the pairing.


  ### How do I connect Roku to Soundbar?


  1. Use the included power cable with your Roku TV and connect your Roku Soundbar to a wall outlet.

  2. With the included cable, connect your soundbar to the ARC port on your TV.
